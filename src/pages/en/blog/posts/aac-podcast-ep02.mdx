Welcome to this podcast episode! Speech-language pathologists Wen-Hsin and Hsiao-Ting share clinical stories about using AAC. They believe every child has the potential to learn to communicate. They are also grateful to the children who have taught them along the way, helping them become better clinicians.

They hope this episode helps more people understand AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) and work together to help children find a way to communicate.
