Happy Mother's Day to all the moms!

Mother's Day calls for AAC too!

Core vocabulary for Mother's Day: **mom, I, love, you, thank you, help**

The power of core vocabulary is that just a few words can be combined into so many messages!

---

## 1. Say It to Mom

The most important part of AAC instruction is meaningful communication. Use AAC to say out loud:

"Mom, I love you. Thank you."

---

## 2. Shared Book Reading

AAC users also have the right to learn to read!

Mother's Day picture books: *Guess How Much I Love You*, *The Runaway Bunny*, *Hug*

While reading together, narrate the story verbally and model vocabulary on the AAC system. Start by modeling just one word at a time, such as "love" or "you."

---

## 3. Make a Mother's Day Card

You can create with AAC too!

Organize and express your thoughts in writing — for example: "Mom I love you," "Thank you for helping me."

The teacher or communication partner can help write these on the card.
