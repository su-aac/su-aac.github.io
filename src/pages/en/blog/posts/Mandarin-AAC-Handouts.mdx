Thank you, Bilingue AAC, for inviting me to collaborate on translating these awesome AAC handouts!

These Mandarin Chinese AAC handouts can be printed for parents or posted in therapy rooms.

The handouts cover:
- **Types of AAC** — What forms does AAC come in?
- **How to begin with AAC?** — Getting started guide
- **Aided language stimulation (modeling)** — What is it and why it matters
- **What does modeling look like?** — Practical examples
